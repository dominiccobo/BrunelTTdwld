About

This system allows you to download your timetable in the two most common calendar formats known:

  • CSV - a comma separated value table: used by most Calendar systems including Google.
  • ICS - iCalendar™ mostly used by Apple Inc. systems, but compatible with most modern agendas.
After you export you will be required to import into your default calendar manager.

How to Use

  1. In the extension click on Open Timetable
  2. Enter your credentials on the login page*
  3. On the left hand side click on My Timetable
  4. Now select a single week (see warning #1)
  5. Change the type of report to List Timetable (see warning #2)
  6. On the extension select your download format and click Download

Warning #1 You must select a single week, date ranges exceeding the length of a week will not work!

Warning #2 You must use List Timetable as the type of report!

Tips & Recommended Practises

Run this every week late on Sunday as the University sometimes may change schedules last moment.

Google Calendar users: create an additional calendar, this allows you to set custom items such as event colours & notifications.
